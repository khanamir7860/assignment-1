Ingredients :
Paneer Butter Masala ingredients include paneer (200–250 grams, cubed), butter (2–3 tablespoons), tomatoes (3–4 medium, pureed), cashew nuts (10–12, soaked and blended), fresh cream (2–3 tablespoons), onion (1 medium, optional), ginger-garlic paste (1 tablespoon), turmeric powder (¼ teaspoon), red chili powder (1 teaspoon), coriander powder (1 teaspoon), garam masala (½ teaspoon), kasuri methi (1 teaspoon, crushed), salt (to taste), sugar (½–1 teaspoon, optional), water (as needed), and cooking oil (1 tablespoon, optional).
