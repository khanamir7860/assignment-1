Masala Omelette Ingredients (in one paragraph, comma-separated): Eggs (2–3), onion (1 small, finely chopped), tomato (1 small, finely chopped), green chili (1, finely chopped), coriander leaves (a handful, finely chopped), red chili powder (½ teaspoon), turmeric powder (¼ teaspoon), garam masala (¼ teaspoon, optional), salt (to taste), black pepper powder (a pinch), oil or butter (1–2 teaspoons for frying).
