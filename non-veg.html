Ingredient :
Chicken Biryani ingredients include chicken (500 grams, bone-in preferred), basmati rice (2 cups), onions (2 large, thinly sliced), tomatoes (2 medium, chopped), yogurt (½ cup), ginger-garlic paste (2 tablespoons), green chilies (2–3, slit), fresh coriander leaves (a handful, chopped), mint leaves (a handful, chopped), whole spices (4–5 cloves, 2 green cardamoms, 1 black cardamom, 1 bay leaf, 1 small cinnamon stick), red chili powder (1 teaspoon), turmeric powder (½ teaspoon), biryani masala (1½ teaspoons), salt (to taste), ghee or oil (4–5 tablespoons), lemon juice (1 tablespoon), saffron strands (a few, soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk), and water (as needed for boiling rice).
